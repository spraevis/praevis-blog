# S. Praevis — Twitter / X Style Guide

## Tone
- ultra-short
- no hashtags
- no emojis
- steady and calm
- avoid judgment words
- no “signals”, “tips”, or “guides”

## Tweet Templates

### 1) Observation
Short observation.  
Short conclusion.

### 2) Probability Update
Probability update:  
+X% or −X%.  
One-sentence reason.

### 3) Noise Removal
What turned out to be noise.  
What remains in the model.

### 4) Three-line Rhythm
Thought.  
Refinement.  
Conclusion.

## Example Tweets
- Data changed. Probability did not.  
- Removed noise. Thinking is simpler now.  
- The model failed. I updated.  
- Small observation, large refinement.  
- Probability increased. Reason — new information.

## Tweet Rules
- 1 idea = 1 tweet
- max 20–25 words
- no threads unless needed
- regularity over length
